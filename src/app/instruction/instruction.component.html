The individual investment yields a guaranteed doubling of the payoff every round. On the other hand, the group investment payoff is dependent on how much other traders have invested in the common pool such that every playersâ€™ contributions are lumped together, multiplied by 4, and then distributed back evenly. The person with the highest individual payoff at the end wins!
